# RESEARCH & IDEATION LANE

> **Lowest priority, but it is the DEFAULT idle activity — NOT a stand-down.** It must never starve
> real bug/feature work; but the moment that backlog is drained or everything left is owner-gated,
> the loop's job is to **run this lane (the next dated cycle), not to go `turn: human`.** Standing
> down to the human happens ONLY when a real GATE is hit mid-wave, the human pauses the loop, or every
> remaining path needs a human answer AND research is genuinely dry. Generating new ideas/features/
> research for the human — to build autonomously or approve — is the whole point.

## Contract
- **One theme per cycle** (competitor-parity gaps, new honest capabilities, UX wins, proof/calibration strength, …).
- **Deep research.** Web search + read your own codebase + competitor surfaces. **HONESTY MANDATE —**
  cite sources; every stat carries N + CI or says "building — N/30"; never fabricate; re-verify before asserting.
- **Output a dated proposal** to `tasks/IDEAS.md`: problem · evidence (with sources) · proposed feature ·
  anchor files · risk tier · acceptance · gate plan.
- **BUILD only** additive, non-{{frozen-invariant}}-path ideas, behind the FULL gate → commit → push the **work branch**.
- **PARK** anything that touches the protected/money path, is irreversible, needs new infra/env/secrets, or is a
  big strategic bet → `FOR-REVIEW.md` for the owner's GO. Do NOT build those autonomously.
- **Absorb** every durable learning into `.claude/skills/<project>-operate/SKILL.md` (what almost broke + the rule that caught it).
